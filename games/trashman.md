# trashman

![Trashman title logo.png](../.gitbook/assets/migrated\_media-Trashman\_title\_logo.png)

### Introduction

A mysterious life form has taken control of the "Junk Sector" space colony. Ares, an ultramodern robot prototype, has been sent to investigate and destroy the threat. Annihilate countless robots and take on gigantic bosses in this classic side-scroller.
